About Me

 

An Italian kid based out of Genoa (IT), I’m someone who’s most at home when I’m creating things. My interests are eclectic, and they include cooking, nature, photography and music. I used to work as a sound engineer and computer musician, but today I am a web developer.

My career in web development started in September 2017. Motivated to pick up new skills at a fast pace, I’m currently focused most on CSS, SVG, and React JS.

I’m curious by nature, and because I learn easily, I’ve taken to the technology field very smoothly. A hard worker, I like to think of myself as crafty, coming up with solutions to problems in novel ways!
